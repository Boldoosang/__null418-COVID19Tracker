• Disease Info/ History

    • COVID 19 is a form of Coronavirus disease. It is highly infectious and is the impetus for the ongoing 2019-20 coronavirus pandemic. The disease is commonly characterised by symptoms of fever, cough and shortness of breath. Other symptoms include muscle pain, sore throat, loss of smell or taste and abdominal pain.

    • COVID 19 was discovered in Wuhan, China in December of 2019. The virus is mainly spread through the close contact of humans.
